U.S.Crime Statistics

In an effort to comprehend the types of personal crimes committed nationally, our team analyzed data from the Bureau of Justice Statistics over a 10 year span (2009 - 2018). We evaluated the volume of personal crimes by region, types of crimes, relationship between victim and offender, types of weapon used during the crimes, and the number of injuries that occurred as a result of the crime.
